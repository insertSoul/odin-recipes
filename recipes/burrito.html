Burrito

Description

A great snack you can take on the move if need be. Originating from mexico this dish can have many different additions or substitutions, this is just the start!

Extra meat of veggies could easily be added. For best results toast afterwards.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Black beans
  • Garlic
  • Chipotle paste
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Tortilla wraps
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa
  • Cheese
  • Cumin
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put the rice in cold water, bring to the boil and cook for 20mins at a simmer, optionally add coriander and lime juice to the rice
  2. Heat the black beans in a pan with garlic, chipotle paste, cumin and sugar, after 1 minute add the tomatoes and cook for 20 minutes until thick
  3. Once everything is cooked, heat your Tortilla for a few seconds and add in the rice, beans, salsa, guacamole and cheese
  4. Roll it all up making sure to fold in the sides before to keep in the fillings
  5. Put it in the oven for a few minutes to get all the cheeses to melt
