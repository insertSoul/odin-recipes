Lentil Curry

Description

This is a nice simple and quick to make recipe that can easily be modified with additional vegetables for a good variety

Simply serve with rice or Naan-bread

  • red lentils
  • coconut milk
  • vegetable stock
  • tinned tomatoes
  • Spices:
    • curry powder
    • cumin
    • garam masala
    • red chilli poweder
  • Aromatics:
    • ginger
    • chili peppers
    • turmeric
